THE CONCEPT

Orangerie Garden + Home is a retail experience that combines beautiful plants and garden objects with chic and charming treasures for the home. First brought into fashion in the 17th Century, the idea of an orangeire was to create a space to house exotic plants over the winter, particularly citrus and fruit trees. Often, these structures were architecturally significant and grand confections. The concept behind Orangerie Garden + Home is to create a retail environment that evokes that sense of style by curating an assortment of indoor and outdoor plants, items for memorable entertaining, and unique accents for the garden and home.
